About Makamithi

Your trusted partner in agricultural excellence

Our Story

Makamithi is among the top leading agricultural inputs distributor in the country. With over 25 years' experience in the industry – 'We are large enough to have the expertise but small enough to care!'

We have a network of logistics consisting over 20 plus vehicles and distribution chain across the region. We have a network of retail branches which act as distribution centers. All branches have qualified agronomists and veterinary specialists to support local farmers, ensuring they carry out sustainable farming practices.

Our mission is to improve livelihoods in the markets we operate; whilst building sustainable, meaningful relationships with our customers and partners. This is through a passion for customer service, an enhanced distribution capacity, availability of quality inputs and products, and competitive pricing.
